Welcome to Baraat

Why Baraat?

Food, they say, is nothing but a celebration of flavours, spices, palates and emotions, without prejudices, borders or mundanity, just like a wedding baraat. And so, Baraat came to be conceptualised as a celebration of regional foods of India, built on the Indian cloud kitchen concept for delivery and catering.

Baraat takes the authentic regional recipes from Punjab, Rajasthan, Bengal, Goa and Kerala, beyond the mundane preparations and brings a menu as distinctive, yet traditional as the presentation. The expert chefs at Baraat have ensured use of only high class & authentic ingredients so that the correct culinary flavours are delivered.
